Practice and Training

A character has both practice sessions and training sessions. Practice sessions can be used to improve the characters skills, while Training sessions can be used to improve thier attributes. A character will typically gain 3 practice sessions and one training session each time they gain a level.

Practice

To practice a skill you need one or more practice sessions and a suitable teacher. This can be an adept mob, a mob with the teaching skill or another character with the teaching skill.
  • practice
  • learn
  • teach
One you have passed the adept rank in a skill, it becomes harder to improve it through practice.

Training

To train you need one or more training sessions.
  • train
With training you can:
  • Improve your strength, intelligence, wisdom, dexterity or constitution.
  • Improve your hits, mana or move.
  • Gain additional practice sessions.
  • Improve your sanity.
