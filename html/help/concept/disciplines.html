Disciplines

Magical Disciplines are highly specialized knowledge about a certain type of magic. Easy or basic spells usually don't require such a knowledge.
Now how can they be attained? The easiest way is to get yourself a Grimoire and begin to explore the world. Usually they are hidden in mystic places or are rewards for dangerous quests.
If you found one, you'll automatically write it into your grimoire. By reading your grimoire you can find out what you already got.
