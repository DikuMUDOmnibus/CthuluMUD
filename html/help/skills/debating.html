Debating [Skill]


With that skill you can begin a discussion about any other academic skill (no dabating about dodge or dual wield!).  Such a discussion will take some time and might tire you down, but if you succees, there is a chance you can learn something about the skill you were talking about.  If you lose the debate, you might feel a bit unsure about what you really know of that skill.
Not everyone is able or willing to debate.
Not everyone can understand your language.
You'll gain most, if you try to debate with someone, who is almost as good in debate as you are.

Syntax:
  debate <character> <topic>
