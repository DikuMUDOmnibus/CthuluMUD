Mind Meld [Spell]


The mind meld spell is a powerful psionic attack upon the mental faculties of an opponent.  If successful, the spell delivers a powerful damaging blow, as well as temorarily crippling the mind of the opponent.  Affected victims will be at -5 intelligence and will have difficulty performing tasks that require deep concentration until the melding wears off.

Syntax:
cast 'mind meld' <character>
