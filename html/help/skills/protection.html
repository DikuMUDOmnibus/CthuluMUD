Protection [Spell]


The Protection Good and Protection Evil spells reduce the damage taken from any attack by a creature of the specified alignment by one quarter.
The Sanctuary spell reduces the damage taken by the character from any attack by one half.
The Globe of Protection spell surrounds you with a protective globe.
These spells may be used simultaneously for cumulative effect.

Syntax:
cast 'protection good'
cast 'protection evil'
cast 'sanctuary' <character>
cast 'globe of protection'
