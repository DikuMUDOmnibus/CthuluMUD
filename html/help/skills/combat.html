Combat Magic [Spell]


These are the powerful spells with which mages attack their opponents. The higher level the spell, the more damage it causes, but also the longer and harder it is to cast.

In using acid blast, the caster summons a ball of powerful acid and fires it at the victim, causing severe acid burns and powerful damage.

Casting burning hands causes the caster's hands to light aflame for several seconds. While on fire, the caster presses his or her hands against the victim's flesh, causing burning damage.

When casting colour spray, a mage fires blasts of colour from their fingertips, dazzling the enemy, and causing damage with the speed and power of the colourful particles.

The spell fireball is just as it seems, wherin the caster summons an enormous ball of flames and launches it at the victim, with both the impact of the ball and the intense heat causing powerful amounts of damage.

Using the lightning bolt spell causes the mage to fire bursts of electricity from their outstretched fingers, similar in power to a strike of nature's furious lightning.

Shocking grasp combines the basics of burning hands and lightning, causing the caster's hands to become electrified and deliver a powerful shock to the victim when grabbed and held.

Casting magic missile releases a volley of purple missiles, formed of energy which impact the target, causing damage.
Zap is a smaller version of magic missile, firing only one, relatively weak missle at the target. It is, however, far easier to cast than magic missle, making it a good starting spell for newer players.
The magic zapper spell is a stronger, more powerful version of magic missle, firing as many missiles, with each one at twice the intensity. It does great deals of damage, but is harder to cast.
 

Syntax:
  cast 'acid blast'     <victim>
  cast 'burning hands'  <victim>
  cast 'colour spray'   <victim>
  cast 'fireball'       <victim>
  cast 'lightning bolt' <victim>
  cast 'shocking grasp' <victim>
  cast 'magic missile'  <victim>
  cast 'zap'  <victim>
  cast 'magic zapper'  <victim>
