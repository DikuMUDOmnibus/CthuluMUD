Universality / Permanence / Consistence [Spell]


Universality, cast upon an item, will allow it to be taken with the caster in and out of their dreams.
Permanence makes a magic object virtually indestructible.
Consistence makes rotting objects stable - even after your own death.

Syntax:
  cast 'universality' <object>
  cast 'permanence' <object>
  cast 'consistence' <object>
