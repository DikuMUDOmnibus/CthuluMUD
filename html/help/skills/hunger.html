Gnawing Hunger / Burden of Blubber / Slender Lines [Spell]


Gnawing hunger empties the stomach of the target, making them very hungry and weakened.
Burden of blubber causes the target to balloon up, fattening them and causing a variety of negative effects that accompany obesity.
Slender lines removes excess blubber from the target's body, slimming them down. (and often curing a great deal of self consciousness.)

Syntax:
  cast 'gnawing hunger' <character>
  cast 'burden of blubber' <character>
  cast 'slender lines' <character>
