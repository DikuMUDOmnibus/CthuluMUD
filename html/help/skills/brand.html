Brand [Spell]


The brand spell gives a magic user the chance to put a random elemental brand (Flame, Frost, Acid, Lightning) upon a weapon.  There is a risk that the weapon will be destroyed, but if branded successfully, the brand is permanent.  Branding only works on weapons without a current brand flag.  High level gods have the ability to create weapons with multiple branding flags...however, such weapons are *never* for use by mortals.....

syntax: cast brand <weapon>
