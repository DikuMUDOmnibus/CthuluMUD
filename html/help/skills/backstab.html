Backstab / Assassinate / Blackjack


Backstab is the favored attack of  thieves, murderers, and other rogues.  It can be used with any weapon type, but is most effective with piercing weapons. The damage inflicted by a backstab is determined by the attacker's level, his weapon skill, his backstab skill, and the power of his opponent.

Assassinate is the master skill of silent & fast killing.  It will give you a huge advantage on your first attack - even more, when you're using a gun type weapon (sniping rifles prefered).

Blackjack is a fast way to knock an enemy out with the first blow.  It's extremely powerful but only does stun damage.

Syntax:
  backstab <character>
  assassinate <character>
  blackjack <character>
