Mask Self / Incognito [Spell]


This spell gives you nearly the full appearance of the target mob.  Anyone casually walking into the room you occupy, or scanning from a distance will not be able to distinguish you from a mob.  Looking directly at you will still reveal your real equipment (if you're not superior in that spell), so the masking isn't perfect.
However, it is very good for avoiding detection.
If you cast Incognito, the victim to mask as does not need to be in your room.

Syntax:
cast 'mask self' <character>
cast 'incognito' <character>
