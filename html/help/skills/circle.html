Circle [Skill]


Circle is a skill learned by advanced thieves, murderers, and other rogues. It can be used with any weapon type, but is most effective with piercing weapons. This skill allows the practicer to slip out of sight of his opponent briefly, and launch a surprise attack upon him from behind. Needless to say this is very difficult to accomplish, and thus it only thieves ever learn this skill.
Circle only works when the victim is either blind or is not focusing directly on you in combat.  Fighting in a group is an excellent way to get circle attacks to work effectively.

syntax: circle <victim>
