Scribe [Skill]


The scribe command allows a mage to attempt to create scrolls of various spells.  Scribing a spell requires several things.  First, the spell must be known to the mage, and it must be a spell that is allowed to be scribed. There is a monetary cost, a mana cost, and component requirements for scribing spells.  First, you must have sheets of vellum, each spell requires a fresh sheet.  Depending on the particular spell, there may also be a second material component required, but this will be a rare occurence.
Scrolls will eventually dry up and crumble to dust if not used.

Syntax:
  scribe <spell>
