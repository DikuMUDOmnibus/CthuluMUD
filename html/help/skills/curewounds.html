Cure Wounds [Spell]


Using these spells, the caster summons forth powerful energies from the natural world and imbues them into the target, aiding in the healing process naturally used by the body, closing wounds and soothing pain. The higher levels spells retore more life, but cost more mana and take longer to complete.

Syntax:
cast 'cure light' <character>
cast 'cure serious' <character>
cast 'cure critical' <character>
cast 'heal' <character>
