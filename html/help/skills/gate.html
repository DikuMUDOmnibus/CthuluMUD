Gate [Spell]


The gate spell is a powerful transportation magic that opens up a portal between your character and another person or creature somewhere else in the world.  This portal will transport you and any pet you might have, but not other members of your group.  Monsters recieve a save against gate, and monster or players more than 3 levels higher than you can not be gated to at all.  God rooms, private rooms, and no recall rooms cannot be gated to, and no recall rooms cannot be gated out of.  Finally, any god or hero is also immune to gate, as well as is player who has nosummon set.
