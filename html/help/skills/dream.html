True Dreaming / Rude Awakening / Recurring Dream / Enchanted Sleep [Spell]


Pulling the target fully into their dream, true dreaming causes the caster to see their dreams as reality, and their former 'real world' becomes a thing of dreams..
Rude awakening sends a dreaming player abruptly back into their reality.
Casting the recurring dream spell sends an awakened player back to the last point they were dreaming from.
Enchanted sleep evokes a deep sleep on the target, deeper than the Sleep spell. This sleep is so strong, in fact, that the target will immediatly be sent into the dream world. Normally, nightmares don't happen whith this spell.

Syntax:
  cast 'true dreaming' <character>
  cast 'rude awakening' <character>
  cast 'recurring dream' <character>
  cast 'enchanted sleep' <character>
