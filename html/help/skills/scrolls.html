Scrolls/Staves/Wands [Skill]


Magical items require training to use properly.  If your character lacks the necessary skill to use an item, he will fail, possibly destroying it.  The item skills are as follows:
 
Skill Object Command
scrolls The reading of magical scrolls and books  recite <object> [<target>]
staves The use of staves and similar devices brandish <object> [<target>]
wands The use of wands and similar items zap <object> [<target>]
