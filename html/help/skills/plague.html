Plague / Pestillence [Spell]


This spell afflicts the target with a powerful disease, reducing it's fighting abilities and afflicting it with a more powerful venom than poison would. If the plague is strong enough, it can be spread to other players.
Pestillence is even worse since it affects large sections of a whole area immediately.

Syntax:
  cast 'plague' <character>
  cast 'pestillence'
