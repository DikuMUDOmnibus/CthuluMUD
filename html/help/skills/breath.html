Breath Magic [Spell]


These spells allow a powerful mage do damage to an enemy by breathing out a blast of one of these five elements. Try them all, certain enemies may be vulnerable to different elements. The Gas Breath spell hits all mobs in the room, but not the caster.

Syntax:
  cast 'fire breath'      <victim>
  cast 'frost breath'     <victim>
  cast 'acid breath'      <victim>
  cast 'lightning breath' <victim>
  cast 'gas breath'
