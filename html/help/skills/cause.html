Cause Wounds [Spell]


These are spells which cause a burst of pain and damage to the victim's internal organs, injuring them from the inside out.
The more powerful the damaging spell, the more pain and the more it tears the victim apart, but the harder it is and longer it takes to cast.

Syntax:
cast 'cause light' <target>
cast 'cause serious' <target>
cast 'cause critical' <target>
cast 'harm' <target>
