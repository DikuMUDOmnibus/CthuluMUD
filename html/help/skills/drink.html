Burning Thirst / Free Grog / Ghastly Sobriety [Spell]


Burning thirst causes the victim to lose a great deal of the fluid in their body, drying them out and causing great thirst.
Free grog causes the target to immediatly become stinking drunk. (Feel free to cast this one on me anytime.)
Ghastly sobriety spell is removing all alcohol from the blood and brain of the target, they immediatly recover their senses and lose their drunkeness.

Syntax:
  cast 'burning thirst' <character>
  cast 'free grog' <character>
  cast 'ghastly sobriety' <character>
