Portal [Spell]


Creating a rift in space itself, the mage can open a doorway to a distant location. The target must be a living creature or player, and must be within 3 levels above the caster's or the spell will fail. Some mobs / rooms cannot be portalled to.
Portals open two way gates that last for some time, and so are useful for getting in and out of sticky situations.

Syntax:
  cast 'portal' <character>
