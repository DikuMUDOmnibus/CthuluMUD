Chaos [Spell]


Chaos is a spell available to those who delve into the chaotic magics. Actually, it is more a rather simple spell to execute, but hard to protect from. Once the caster unleashes this spell, he has opened a channel to the pure magic forces from another plane which simple rip through him, and tear through everything in the room, doing damage.
Chaos does damage in a multitude of ways, since the magics from other planes are very unpredictable.

Syntax:
  cast 'chaos' <character>
