Shield / Stone Skin / Iron Skin [Spell]


These are all spells which summon strength from within the caster, providing mystical protection and lowering (improving) the caster's armor class. The better you are at the spell and the higher your level, the greater the protection.
The shield spell summons a shield of energy which surrounds the caster, absorbing some of the incoming damage and reducing injury.
Stone skin is a spell which weaves fibers of the very rock into the caster's skin, making it far harder to slice or bruise them and greatly reducing damage taken. Iron skin is a similiar, but more powerful spell.

Syntax:
cast 'shield'
cast 'stone skin'
cast 'iron skin'
