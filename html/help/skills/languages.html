Languages [Skill]


For those willing to learn, there are many languages which can be used to communicate in the Realm. Learning new languages will allow you to speak to others who talk that language, and will allow you to understand what they say in return. The better you are at a language, the easier it becomes to communicate.
Languages which can be learned, so far, include: English, Spanish, French, German, Gaelic, Polish, Italian, Chinese, Japanese, Hebrew, Arabic, Greek, Latin, Heiroglypics, Old English, Romany, Stygian, Atlantean, and Cthonic.
Some of these, of course, are long dead tongues used by civilizations long wiped out... It becomes far harder to find a teacher for such languages.
