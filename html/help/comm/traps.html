Traps


Traps skill allows you to lay, disarm, identify traps.  There are the following types of traps:
BLADE trap: causes damage
TANGLE trap: entangles & binds a victim
DART trap: shoots a poisonous dart
MYSTIC trap: the trap is only triggered by persons close to your level
MAGIC trap: teleports the victim

Synatax:
  trap
  trap lay <type>
  trap disarm
  trap info
  trap trigger
