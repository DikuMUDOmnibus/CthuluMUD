Petname


If you got a pet, you can rename it at will (as long as it's not sentient -> you can't rename a human follower!).  The name is at your decision, but unfitting names will result in the pet's death - vulgar names result in your DELETION.

Syntax:
  petname <name / short / long> <new string>
 
  PETNAME short Fido the dog
  PETNAME long Fido the dog is here.
  PETNAME name fido dog
