Therapy


In this dark, demonic realm, you will occasionally encounter something that is not fit for mortal eyes to see, and that mortal minds cannot cope with. Eviscerated, mangled corpses, beings which are beyond the realm of understanding.. Whenever you see such a thing, and the fear in your heart bursts forth, it will slowly chip away at your sanity leaving you a wreck of a being, mentally inept. The only way to undo it is to find a therapist, one who can help you work through your problems. When you find one, type therapy to see what kinds of therapy you can receive.

Syntax:
  therapy
  therapy <type>
