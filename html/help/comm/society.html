Societiy


Societies are a great way to help protect yourself in a player killing environment.  They also allow you to become more involved with the world you are playing in.  If you have an interest in forming a new society, post a note to immortal with your ideas and thoughts regarding a proposed society.  We'll post a note back to you with the details of society creation and rulership here on Cthulhumud.  If you have an  interest in joining an already existing clan, talk to a member of that clan, surely they'll be willing to help anyone interested in their society.
Here is a quick reference list:

Syntax:
  society
  society list
  society list <type>
  society info
  society info <id>
  society members <id>
  society invite <character> <id>
  society join <id>
  society expel <character> <id>
  society resign <id>
  society promote <character> <id>
  society demote <character> <id>
  society test <character> <id>
  society auth <character> <id>
  society auth <character> <id> <authority>
  society bank <id> <action>
  society foe <character> <id>
  society pardon <character> <id>
  society tell <id> <message>
  ? <id> <message>
  society research <character>
  society sign <id>
