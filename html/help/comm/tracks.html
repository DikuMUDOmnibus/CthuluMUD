Tracks

This commands makes you character search for tracks that may have been left by other characters and mobiles.

Syntax:

  tracks
  track name

Tracks on its own searches the room for tracks and tells you about all the ones it finds.

Tracks with the name of a set of tracks starts a tracking activity (enter stand to stop it). Your character will search the room for the tracks and if its finds them it will follow them into the next room where it will search for tracks again.

Tracks can be under OLC to make a mob follor a guide track. The mob simply has to issue track _track_name and it will follow the named guide track and execute the specified scripts.
