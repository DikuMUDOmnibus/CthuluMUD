Scan / Exits


Exit: Tells you the visible exits of the room you are in.  Not all exits are visible. You can't use the 'bump' technique to find hidden exits. Searching might reveal a hidden exit, but some use levers, buttons, switches to open them. Autoexit manages the use of that command for you.

Scan: In addition to the features, exit command offers, scan looks for mobs in the surrounding rooms. As long as it's not dark of foggy, it is able to report, who's there.

Syntax: exits
              scan
