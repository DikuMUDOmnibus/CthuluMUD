Professions


This command allows you to display all of the defined professions and details about each one.  It shows the skills available at each level of that profession as well as some numbers concerning new characters created in that profession.
You can also use it to change your profession.  This costs 3 practice points and can only be done once you have reached 3rd level in your current profession.  When you change to a new profession you gain a beginners rank in all of it's base (level 0) skills (unless you already have a higher score in them).

Syntax:
  prof list
  prof list '<profession>'
  prof change '<profession>'
