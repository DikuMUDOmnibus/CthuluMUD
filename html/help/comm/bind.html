Bind / Free / Rob / Carry


By turning off the autokill switch, and removing your weapons, you will attempt to knock your opponent out instead of killing them. Once this is accomplished, if you're wielding a bondage object, you can bind that player. They will be unable to move, speak, or do anything else. While in this state, of course, they are very vulnerable, and can be carried around, or robbed of any object in their inventory. Remember that to some this action could be construed to be as bad as pk, so bind consciously, and with RP reasons only. And remember that bound players cannot eat or drink, so be kind and free them when you're done, else they may starve or die of thirst, or be easily killed by a passer-by.
Remember, just like with PK, everything must have an RP reason or it will be dealt with.

Synatax:
  bind <character>
  rob <character> <object>
  carry <character>
  drop <character>
  free <character>
