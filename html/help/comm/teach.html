Teach


If you have the teaching skill, this lets you teacha skill to another player who is following you.  Count is the number of practice sessions they are going to use to learn the skill from you.  Teach works best when you effectiuve skill (the skill being taught multiplied by your teaching skill/100) is greater than your pupils skill.

Note that a cash transaction will occur, with the pupil paying you for your teaching.  If you wish a different price, you should negotiate it separatly before hand.

Syntax:
  teach <player> <skill> <count>
